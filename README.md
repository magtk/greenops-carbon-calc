# GreenOps Carbon Calculator

Educational browser-based calculator for estimating the carbon footprint of selected digital activities, online meetings and simple cloud/hosting scenarios.

All calculations are performed locally in the browser. No data is sent or stored.

Part of MagT's educational resources.

# Disclaimer

This calculator is provided for educational and illustrative purposes only.

The calculations are based on simplified assumptions, publicly available estimates, and generalized emission factors. Results should be treated as approximate indicators rather than precise measurements.

Users should independently verify all assumptions, methodologies, and results before relying on them in any professional context.

This tool is intended to support learning, discussion, and awareness of GreenOps and sustainability concepts.
